Veg 65

Description

Vegetable 65 is an easy-to-make and delicious Hyderabadi starter is prepared using cauliflower, mushrooms, and potatoes marinated with spices and flavoured with chili sauce, soya sauce and vinegar. This appetizer recipe is an amazing combination of vegetables and spicy sauces. This is a crispy vegetarian dish that people would love to have on occasions like kitty parties, birthdays and anniversaries.

Actually the name vegetable 65 is because, here mixed vegetables are cooked 65 style. Now what is 65 style? Somebody told me, It's an Indian style, which means it has 65 ingredients. But don't worry, I have made it a bit simpler for you and used very less ingredients with the same great taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium cauliflower
  • 4 pieces ginger
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 red chilli
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 small garlic
  • 2 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon light soya sauce
  • 2 cup virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon water

    • For Marination

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoon corn flour
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste

    • For Garnishing

  • 1 handful coriander leaves

    • For The Main Dish

  • 5 medium mushroom
  • 2 medium potato

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables into medium size using a chopping board and keep them aside. Take one mixing bowl and add all purpose flour, corn flour, salt, garam masala, coriander powder, ginger and garlic paste and mix it well. Then add some water to form a thick paste.
  2. Add all the chopped vegetables in the paste and mix well. Let the vegetables marinated for about 2 hours so that the veggies are properly covered with the paste. Now put a deep frying pan on high flame and add oil in it.
  3. Deep fry all the vegetables till golden brown. Then take out the veggies and place on a paper towel to remove the extra oil from it. Now take another frying pan and and add 2 tbsp oil in it and let it heat for some time over medium flame. Meanwhile, crush the red chillies with a little water and make a smooth red chilli paste with pestle and mortar
  4. Now add finely chopped onions in oil along with finely chopped ginger and garlic and saute for 2-3 minutes, until the onion becomes translucent and pinkish in colour. Add 1 tsp of red chilli paste in it. Also, add the tomato sauce, vinegar, soya sauce and salt carefully as soya sauce also contain salt so keep a check on taste and add 1 tsp of chilli sauce as well to the mixture.
  5. Take one bowl separately and add 1 tbsp of corn flour in it and dilute it with water. Now, add it in the sauce prepared to make it thick. Add all fried vegetables to the sauce. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot!
